Report Data Issues

Found incorrect train timings, missing stations, or outdated fare information? You can help!

What You Can Report:

  • Incorrect Train Timings - Wrong departure or arrival times
  • Missing Stations - Stations not listed in our database
  • Wrong Station Names - Spelling errors or incorrect station information
  • Outdated Fare Information - Incorrect ticket prices
  • Missing Train Details - Incomplete train information

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Train Timing ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Feedback Data: When you submit feedback, we collect your name (optional), email address (optional), feedback type, and feedback message.
  • Usage Preferences: Your app settings and preferences for personalized experience.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including features used and time spent.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version.
  • Crash Reports: Automatically collected crash data via Firebase Crashlytics to improve app stability.

2.3 Train Schedule Data

We store Pakistan Railways train schedules, station information, and timing data locally on your device. This data does not contain any personal information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To respond to your feedback and support requests
  • To improve data accuracy based on user reports
  • To send push notifications about train schedule updates (with your permission)
  • To analyze app usage and improve user experience
  • To detect and prevent technical issues and crashes

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase (Google): For analytics, crash reporting, cloud messaging, and remote configuration. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Pakistan Railways: Train schedule data is sourced from Pakistan Railways public information.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your feedback data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database with industry-standard security measures. Train schedule data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about the data we have collected
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your feedback data
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications in app settings

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your feedback data directly through the app at any time:

  1. Open the Train Timing app
  2. Navigate to Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Data
  3. Tap on "Delete My Data"
  4. Confirm deletion

All feedback data submitted from your device will be permanently and immediately deleted from our servers. The app automatically identifies and removes all feedback associated with your device.

Please note that train schedule data stored locally on your device can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

Alternatively, you can email us at droidclics@gmail.com with your deletion request.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications about train schedule updates and important announcements. You can disable notifications in your device settings or app settings at any time.

10. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers (Firebase) for app functionality

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
