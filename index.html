Privacy Policy for Train Timing

Last updated: January 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Train Timing ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for Android devices.

By using Train Timing, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Voluntarily Provide

  • Feedback Information: When you choose to submit feedback through our in-app form, we collect:
    • Your name (optional)
    • Your email address (optional)
    • Feedback type (suggestion, bug report, compliment, etc.)
    • Your feedback message
  • Search History: Train searches you perform are stored locally on your device for quick access to recent searches
  • Favorites: Trains you mark as favorites are stored locally on your device
  • Preferences: App settings like theme preference (light/dark mode), language selection, and notification preferences

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect:
    • Device model and manufacturer
    • Operating system version
    • App version
    • Unique device identifiers (for analytics purposes only)
  • Usage Data: Anonymous information about:
    • Features you use most frequently
    • Time spent in the app
    • Navigation patterns within the app
    • Search queries (train names, routes, stations)
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash reports and diagnostic information to help us identify and fix technical issues

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ We do not collect precise location data or GPS coordinates
  • ❌ We do not access your contacts, calendar, or call logs
  • ❌ We do not access your photos, videos, or other media files
  • ❌ We do not track your browsing history outside the app
  • ❌ We do not collect financial or payment information (booking redirects to official PR website)
  • ❌ We do not collect biometric data
  • ❌ We do not record audio or video

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide App Functionality: Display train schedules, manage favorites, show search history
  • Improve User Experience: Understand how users interact with the app to make improvements
  • Respond to Feedback: Address your questions, suggestions, and bug reports
  • Send Notifications: Deliver train schedule updates, delay alerts, and journey reminders (only if you enable notifications)
  • Develop New Features: Analyze usage patterns to prioritize feature development
  • Technical Support: Diagnose and fix technical issues, crashes, and bugs
  • Maintain Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security vulnerabilities
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: Meet legal and regulatory requirements

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Firebase (Google LLC)

  • Purpose: Cloud database for storing train schedules and user feedback submissions
  • Data Shared: Feedback submissions (if you choose to provide them), anonymous crash reports
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Location: Data may be stored on servers in the United States

4.2 Pakistan Railways Official Website

  • Purpose: Ticket booking functionality
  • Note: When you click "Book Ticket" or similar buttons, you are redirected to the official Pakistan Railways website. Their privacy policy and terms apply to any information you provide on their site. We do not collect or store any booking information.
  • Data Shared: None. The redirect is handled by your device's browser.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Local Storage: Most data (favorites, search history, preferences) is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage
  • Cloud Storage: Only feedback submissions are stored in Firebase Realtime Database
  • No Third-Party Analytics: We use minimal Firebase Analytics for basic app performance monitoring

5.2 Security Measures

  • Data transmission uses HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Firebase security rules restrict unauthorized access
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No storage of sensitive personal information (passwords, payment details, etc.)

5.3 Data Retention

  • Feedback Submissions: Retained for 2 years or until you request deletion
  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you clear app data or uninstall
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized; retained for 14 months
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 90 days
